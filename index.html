About the System

Health Center Appointment & Patient Queuing System

The Health Center Appointment and Patient Queue System is a web-based platform designed to improve healthcare service efficiency by allowing patients to schedule appointments online and monitor their queue status before visiting the health center.

This system helps reduce waiting time, organize patient flow, and provide a better healthcare experience through a digital queue monitoring system.
